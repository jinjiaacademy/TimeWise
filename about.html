About TimeWise

The Lyubishev Time-Tracking Method

TimeWise is inspired by the time management system developed by Alexander Lyubishev, a Russian entomologist and philosopher who lived from 1890 to 1972. Lyubishev meticulously tracked his time for over 50 years, which allowed him to achieve extraordinary productivity in his scientific work while maintaining a balanced life.

The core principles of the Lyubishev method include:

  • Detailed Time Recording: Track all activities throughout the day, including work, study, leisure, and personal time.
  • Categorization: Group activities into meaningful categories to identify patterns and balance.
  • Regular Analysis: Review time usage periodically to optimize productivity and ensure alignment with personal goals.
  • Continuous Improvement: Use insights from tracking to make deliberate choices about how to spend time.

Unlike modern productivity systems that focus on maximizing work output, Lyubishev's approach emphasizes conscious time allocation across all life areas, leading to sustainable productivity and personal fulfillment.

Benefits of Time Tracking

Increased Awareness

Develop a clear understanding of how you actually spend your time versus how you think you spend it.

Better Decision Making

Make informed choices about time allocation based on data rather than perception.

Reduced Time Waste

Identify and eliminate activities that don't contribute to your goals or well-being.

Life Balance

Ensure adequate time for all important life domains: work, relationships, health, and personal growth.

How to Use TimeWise

1. Track Your Time

Use the timer to record your activities as you perform them:

  • Enter the activity name in the input field
  • Select an appropriate category from the dropdown
  • Click "Start Timer" when you begin the activity
  • Click "Stop Timer" when you finish
  • Add optional notes about what you accomplished
  • Click "Save Entry" to record your time

2. Review Your Entries

The "Recent Entries" section shows your latest time records:

  • View activity name, category, duration, and time period
  • Edit entries by clicking on them if you need to make corrections
  • Delete entries that were recorded by mistake

3. Analyze Your Data

Use the Analytics Dashboard to gain insights:

  • Toggle between daily and weekly views
  • Examine time distribution across different categories
  • Track your total time spent on productive activities
  • Identify which categories consume most of your time

4. Use the Calendar

Navigate through your historical data:

  • Browse through months using the navigation arrows
  • Days with recorded activities are marked with a dot
  • Click on any day to view detailed analytics for that specific date
  • The popup shows a breakdown of activities and time distribution

5. Customize Your Experience

Personalize the app to suit your preferences:

  • Toggle between light and dark mode using the switch in the header
  • Your preference will be saved for future sessions

Tips for Effective Time Tracking

Be Consistent

Track your time every day to build a habit and ensure accurate data. Even a few days of missed tracking can skew your analysis.

Be Honest

Record activities as they actually happened, not as you wish they had. The value of time tracking comes from accurate self-awareness.

Be Specific

Use descriptive activity names that will make sense when you review them later (e.g., "Writing project proposal" instead of just "Working").

Review Regularly

Set aside time weekly to analyze your data and reflect on patterns. Monthly and quarterly reviews provide even deeper insights.

Adjust Gradually

Use insights to make small, sustainable changes to your time allocation rather than attempting dramatic overhauls.
